My Background

Before becoming a developer, I have worked for more than 10 years in the advertising industry as a creative planner and an interaction designer.

I have also pursed my passion for digital art.

I completed a Master in the field (read my thesis), and adventured myself as a artist. I even had the honor to receive a few awards.

I have mostly been building interactive installations (C++, Processing and WebGL) and robots with sensors (C and Arduino). Now, I am fascinated by front-end development and would love continuing to tailor beautiful and useful interfaces.
